# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on the code, not the person.
- Assume good intent.
- Disagree technically, not personally.

## Enforcement

Report violations to **administrador@tealquilamos.com**. All reports are treated confidentially.

Maintainers may warn, edit, or remove comments, commits, issues, or pull requests that violate this code, and may temporarily or permanently ban contributors for repeated or severe violations.
